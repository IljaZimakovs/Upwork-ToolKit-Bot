Mid-level Software Developer for Software as Medical Device development in ExSeed Health

Posted yesterday
Worldwide

Company Introduction:
ExSeed Health is Copenhagen and London based pioneering MedTech/Software company specializing in the development of cutting-edge medical devices and mobile applications with a primary focus on treating male infertility. We have introduced a revolutionary microscope device which has enabled thousands of men to test their semen quality from the comfort of their homes. Our first-generation device detects sperm concentration and motility with a simple process involving a smartphone and has won several industry innovation awards.
We have spent considerable efforts building a sophisticated cloud-based digital microscopy platform and in late 2023, we launched our second-generation device, which is a digital microscope for medical professionals in the B2B segment, requiring higher demands for consistent video quality and more advanced parameter analysis. The market reception has exceeded our expectations, and we are now focused on further enhancing our second-generation device to expand its market reach.

About the job:
To support our exciting growth, we are seeking a skilled Mid-level Software Developer to join our Tech Team to support the development of our digital microscope for automated semen analysis. The ideal candidate will have a wish to lead software lifecycle management and a vision to drive the continuous development.
Youre ideally skilled in flutter development with experience in integrating with C++ plugins for camera control of external cameras. You have a desire to be part of the bigger vision of our company and be an integral part of our team.
Key Responsibilities:
Develop and maintain native/web applications using Flutter, with an emphasis on seamless integration with C++ plugins for camera control.
Collaborate closely with cross-functional teams to design, implement, and test new features for our medical devices.
Optimize application performance, particularly in handling high-quality video streams and real-time data analysis.
Conduct unit, integration, and automated testing to ensure the reliability and accuracy of our applications.
Assist in enhancing cloud-based components, leveraging your basic knowledge of cloud computing.
Participate in code reviews and contribute to continuous improvement initiatives within the development team.

Essential Skills:
Strong proficiency in native/web development, with hands-on experience in Flutter.
Strong programming skills in Python, C++, JavaScript, and Dart.
Basic knowledge of cloud computing, particularly in deployment and maintenance.
Ability and motivation to document system requirements, architecture, testing and other regulatory requirements.
Knowledge of unit, integration, and system testing
At least 3 years of experience.
Proficient in written and spoken English

Additional bonus points if you have:
Experience with microscopy, camera control or relevant IoT products.
Familiarity with AWS or other cloud services.
Knowledge of software lifecycle management and software requirement tracking according to IEC 62304
Understanding of MacOS development environments.

General Skills:
An innovative way of thinking
Logical mindset and great attention to detail
A passion for creating awesome products
Positive energy with a love for technology - cultural fit is a big deal for us.
Emotional intelligence / interpersonal skills
Ability to manage projects.

Company Benefits:
Competitive salary in a fast-growing, venture-backed company
Clear path to share-options / ownership
Flexible working hours with the possibility for remote work (2-3 days in the London office per week expected to ensure facetime with the Tech Team.)
Ambitious and collaborative start-up culture

Were extremely agile, innovative, and pragmatic
We have a flexible work culture
Were based in London and Copenhagen (this role can be in London, Copenhagen or Remote).
